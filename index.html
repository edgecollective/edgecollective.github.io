What we do

Our mission is to help people regain control over vital tech. We've focused on:

  • Off-grid farming (e.g. monitoring soil moisture, greenhouse temperature)
  • Flooding alerts (sent via email / sms / satellite modem)
  • Retro-fit / repair / enhance existing infrastructure (e.g. resurrecting bricked 'smart greenhouse' vent motors)
  • Teaching and training (videos, online documentation, in-person workshops)
